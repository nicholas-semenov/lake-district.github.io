Getting Here

By Car

Access via the M6 motorway, exit at junctions 36–40 depending on your destination. Many roads within the park are narrow and can become congested during peak season.

By Train

The West Coast mainline serves the park, with stops at Oxenholme (for Windermere) and Penrith (for the northern lakes and Ullswater). Local bus services connect stations to villages and visitor centres.

By Bus

Several bus services operate within the park, including the "Rambler" and "Coniston Rambler" routes, linking key visitor sites. Using public transport is encouraged to reduce congestion.
