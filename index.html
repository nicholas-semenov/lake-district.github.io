2,362 km² of Beauty

England's largest national park and the second largest in the UK, covering 912 square miles of dramatic landscape.

16 Major Lakes

From Windermere, England's longest lake, to Wastwater, its deepest — each lake tells a different story.

3,200 km of Paths

Hundreds of miles of maintained trails for walkers of all abilities, from lakeshore strolls to challenging fell walks.

Welcome to the Lake District

The Lake District National Park is one of the most visited natural landscapes in the United Kingdom, welcoming over 18 million tourists in 2023. Whether you are seeking peaceful lakeside walks, thrilling water sports, or a connection with literary history, the park offers something for every visitor.
